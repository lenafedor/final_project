RECIPE

1. 

Begin the preparation of the Ascolana olives recipe by pitting the olives using a spiral cut. Then dedicate yourself to the filling, finely chop the celery, the peeled carrot and the peeled onion.

2. 

Pour a couple of tablespoons of oil into a pan and fry the vegetables, add the three types of minced meat, season with salt and pepper and brown the meat well. When it has completely changed colour, i.e. it is cooked, pour in the white wine. and let it evaporate.

3. 

Blend the cooked meat with the vegetables and place everything in a bowl, add the lemon zest, a little nutmeg, the chopped stale breadcrumbs, the cheese and the egg. Knead until you obtain a homogeneous mixture.

4. 

Now stuff each Ascoli olive with a knob of dough and shape it to create a sort of meatball, the pulp of the olives must enclose the filling. Place the olives on a plate.

5. 

At this point, dedicate yourself to the breading. Take three soup plates, put the flour in one, the egg in the second and the breadcrumbs in the third plate.

6. 

Now take one stuffed olive at a time, dip it in the flour, then in the egg and finally in the breadcrumbs. Let the breaded olives rest for about thirty minutes and then repeat the operation, passing each olive again in the flour, then in the egg and breadcrumbs.

7. 

Take a pan and pour plenty of peanut oil into it, as soon as it is hot (170 degrees), fry the olives, a few at a time, until they are perfectly golden. Drain them with a slotted spoon and remove the excess grease by placing them on absorbent kitchen paper. Serve your Ascoli olives nice and hot. But they are also excellent cold.

OLIVE ALL'ASCOLANA

1 hour

fry

From Ascoli Piceno to conquer the world, Ascoli olives are the very symbol of the city. Who can resist the tender and large olives stuffed with meat and breaded crispy on the outside and soft on the inside thanks to a special double breading? Every bite is poetry. Let's see how to prepare them perfectly.

• 

1 celery stick

• 

1 carrot

•

• 

Zest of 1 lemon

•

• 

Extra virgin olive oil to taste

Salt to taste

• 

Black pepper to taste

Flour

Breadcrumbs
