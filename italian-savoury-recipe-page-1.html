RECIPE

1. 

Fry the onion in hot oil, add the rice until toasted, at which point add the stock cube, 2 liters of water, the saffron and a little pepper.

2. 

Look at the cooking time of the rice and turn off the heat one minute before it finishes cooking.

3. 

Add the butter and place everything spread out in a container or on a tray to cool for 10 hours.

4. 

Try to keep the rice moist (not wet), if it dries too much you can moisten it with oil.

5. 

Prepare the ragù: fry the onion, add carrots and cook everything for 2 minutes, add the very fine chopped meat (and possibly the sausage), wait 3 minutes and add peas tomato puree salt pepper sugar, cook everything for about half an hour on a low heat, stirring often.Once cooked, let the sauce cool.

6. 

Prepare the batter: mix the egg with the flour and a little salted water (just under a glass) until you obtain a creamy consistency. We put everything we need in front of us: saffron rice, filling, batter and breadcrumbs.

7. 

Moisten your hands with oil, place a little rice on your palm trying to give it a concave shape, put the filling and a slice of mozzarella inside and close with more rice. Try not to leave air inside, dip it in the batter and then in the breadcrumbs. Now fry until golden brown.
