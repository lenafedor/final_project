ITALIAN STREETFOOD

Italian gastronomy represents the most genuine thing one can expect, after the passing of the centuries, for its simplicity and the use of local raw materials and the healthy and tasty specialties of our land. The dishes that have made the history of Italian cuisine are poor, simple dishes, prepared with very few ingredients. On the other hand, there are many traditional Italian recipes, as are various regional reinterpretations.

Eating well and of quality are characteristics that have always characterized the way of conceiving food. Today it is the traditional recipes, the simple ones, those of our grandmothers that make the history of Italian cuisine.
